The Pepper Foundation

The Pepper Foundation is a 501(c)3 Non-Profit Animal Rescue Organization that rescues primarily special needs and senior animals from high-kill shelters in Los Angeles and surrounding areas. Our mission is to get these animals rehabilitated and adopted into loving forever homes.

The redesign of the Pepper Foundation focused on information architecture due to a lack of organization and consistency. An additional focus was introducing more positive imagery and responsive web design with a goal of encouraging confidence in users and potential pet adoptees.

The Problem Statement

Animal lovers who plan to provide a loving home to elderly or special-needs pets, need an organized website with enhanced transparency about each animal's specific needs and responsibilities that taking care of them entails, in turn allowing owners to make an informed decision, leading them to feel self-confident and supported.
